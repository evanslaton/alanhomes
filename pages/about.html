About the Builder

Established in 1984, Victor Markowitz, President of Alan Homes, Inc. has built quality homes in many of the popular subdivisions in the Toledo Metro area. We are also licensed to build homes in the state of Michigan.

"Dedicated to Your Personal Attention" whether you purchase a spec home or contract with us to custom build your new residence on your lot or ours, we retain open lines of communication at all times.

Alan Homes has participated in many past Parade of Homes including Stone Oak Subdivision, Riverford Subdivision, Apple Blossom, 1000 Oaks, and Shawnee Junction. In addition to the 500 plus homes built under Victor's supervision, over 200 single family lots were developed for sale to area builders and new homebuyers.

Most notably, Waterville Meadows a 157 Lot Subdivision was developed in 1998-2006 as a diversified community for young families as well as soon to be retirees.

Our goal is to build your new home, on your lot or ours, in a professional manner and in a stress-free environment in order to earn your referral which to us is the ultimate compliment.
