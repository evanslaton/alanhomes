About the Builder

Victor Markowitz, President of Alan Homes, Inc., has been a professional custom builder and developer for over 35 years, building hundreds of quality homes in the Toledo Metropolitan Area and in Southeast Michigan.

Victor's customer service philosophy is simple. He dedicates himself to your personal attention throughout all phases of the building process from design and feature selection, through physical construction, and beyond as you settle in and become acquainted with your beautiful custom home.

To accomplish this exemplary level of customer service, you can expect a high level of communications and feedback regarding your design and construction progress. As a custom builder, he is flexible if you wish to modify floor plans and feature selection to suit your individual preferences. Of course, he uses only the highest quality product choices. Finally, his construction team consists only of the most capable and experienced craftsmen to execute the construction plan. The objective here is to offer a stress free building process and a quality home we can all be proud of.

The Alan Homes advantage means more choices, more flexibility, and more attention to your goals and lifestyle requirements. We promise that your home will meet your every expectation today and for years to come.

About the Realtor

Michelle Nieman, Danberry, is a productive, compassionate, courteous and competent real estate professional. To facilitate the new home construction process, Michelle always makes superior customer care her top priority by providing comprehensive real estate services and financial guidance that begin well before new home design and survive long after construction completion and closing.

Michelle has been licensed in Northwest Ohio and Southeast Michigan for thirty years so she knows the market. Through her established alliance with Alan Homes, Inc., she can help in guiding new home buyers through the design, feature selection and construction processes. Do you have a home to sell before you can build? Michelle can furnish you with a professional market analysis to help you decide your price range and optimum locations. With this information in hand, Alan Homes, Inc. can develop an affordable design that will meet your lifestyle goals now and in the future.

Michelle Nieman, Danberry, and Victor Markowitz, Alan Homes, Inc., are dedicated to excellence; using innovative real estate tools and technology to provide the highest quality of service to our clients; above all preserving a belief in people.
